abstract: '<i>Therates ottomerkli </i>Wiesner, 1999 (Coleoptera: Cicindelidae) is reported from Cambodia for the first time, including the first discovery of male specimens. The species is redescribed. A list and an identifica¬tion key to the species of the <i>chennelli </i>group are provided. <i>Therates yunnanensis </i>Wiesner and Xiong, 2024 is transferred from the <i>chennelli </i>group to the <i>tuberosus </i>group. A key to the species of the <i>tuberosus </i>group is also provided.'
