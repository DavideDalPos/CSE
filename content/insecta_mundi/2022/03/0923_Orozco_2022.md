abstract: '<i>Megalurothrips usitatus </i>Bagnall (Thysanoptera: Thripidae), an invasive and highly destructive pest of beans, is recorded for the first time for Honduras. The species is illustrated to aid in its identification. Specimens were collected from <i>Phaseolus vulgaris </i>L. (common bean), <i>Crotalaria juncea </i>L. (sunn hemp), and <i>Melampodium divaricatum </i>(Rich.) DC (butter daisy).'
