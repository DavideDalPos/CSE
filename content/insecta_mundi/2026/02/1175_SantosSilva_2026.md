abstract: '</i>Chlorida inexpectata </i>Martins, Galileo and Limeira-de-Oliveira, 2011 is synonymized with </i>Chlorida denticulata </i>Buquet, 1860 (Coleoptera: Cerambycidae). Notes on </i>Sphallambyx </i>Fragoso, 1982 are provided, with a change of the species-group name of </i>S. superbum </i>(Aurivillius, 1910). New country records are pro¬vided for </i>Sphallambyx mexicanus </i>Galileo and Martins, 2006 and </i>Coleoxestia spinifemorata </i>Fragoso, 1993. New state records are reported for </i>Bebelis occulta </i>(Bates, 1866) and </i>Rhachicolus cristatus </i>Galileo, 1987. </i>Chal¬lustrophis antonkozlovi</i>, new genus, new species, is described from Ecuador. An updated key to the genera of Sphallotrichina is presented.'
