abstract: '<i>Ctenichneumon</i> Tomson, 1894 (Hymenoptera: Ichneumonidae: Ichneumoninae: Ichneumonini) is a moderately diverse parasitoid wasp genus with a broad distribution, including 14 described species in the Nearctic region. Here, I advance our knowledge of Nearctic <i>Ctenichneumon</i> by describing two new species, <i>Ctenichneumon elaiodes</i>, new species, and <i>Ctenichneumon hesperis</i>, new species, from the Huachuca Mountains in southeastern Arizona. Diagnostic notes and specimen images are provided to facilitate identifcation'
