abstract: 'The beetle genus <i>Pharaxonotha </i>Reitter (Coleoptera: Erotylidae: Pharaxonothinae) is found in the cones of cycad genera in the New World, including species of <i>Ceratozamia </i>Brongn., <i>Dioon </i>Lindl., <i>Microcycas </i>(Miq.) A.DC., and <i>Zamia </i>L. (Cycadales: Zamiaceae). Te species diversity of <i>Pharaxonotha </i>with <i>Zamia </i>and <i>Microcycas </i>is reviewed for those occurring in Te Bahamas, Belize, Costa Rica, Greater Antilles, Guatemala, Honduras, Mexico, and southern USA. Twelve species are recognized, and fve new species are described: <i>Pharaxonotha bahamaensis </i>Skelley, Tang and Salzman, new species, <i>P. chavesi </i>Skelley, Tang and Salzman, new species, <i>P. clarkorum </i>Pakaluk, <i>P. confusa </i>Pakaluk, <i>P. esperanzae </i>Chaves and Genaro, <i>P. foridana </i>(Casey), <i>P. grifthi </i>Skelley, Tang and Salzman, new species, <i>P. jamaicensis </i>Skelley, Tang and Salzman, new species, <i>P. kirschii </i>Reitter, <i>P. portophylla </i>Franz and Skelley, <i>P. thomasi </i>Skelley and Tang, and <i>P. zamiaphila </i>Skelley, Tang and Salzman, new species. A key to species of <i>Pharaxonotha </i>found on <i>Zamia </i>and <i>Microcycas </i>in the region is presented.'
