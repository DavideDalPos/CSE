abstract: 'In 2007, a review of the <i>Anthrenus pimpinellae </i>species group was published, a highly significant paper in the context of the current study, in which 16 species were listed. Much work has been carried out on the <i>A. pimpinellae </i>group since then and the species list now stands at 36. Tree new species are described in the current study: <i>Anthrenus callaghanae</i>, <i>Anthrenus citrinus</i>, and <i>Anthrenus strejceki</i>, and images of external and internal features are provided. Additionally, <i>A. kafai </i>Kadej and Háva, 2011 is designated as a new junior synonym of <i>A. favidulus </i>Reitter, 1889. For each species in the review, comments relating to taxonomy and distribution are included. Species are categorized into diferent lineages based on aedeagal structure, some¬times supplemented with other characters. An identification key to the lineages is presented.'
