abstract: '<i>Tricondyla ovaligrossa</i> Horn, 1922 (Coleoptera: Cicindelidae) is recorded from China for the first time. With this new record, all four members of the <i>gestroi</i> species group are now known to occur in China. Photographs, brief descriptions, and an identification key to the Chinese <i>Tricondyla</i> Latreille species are provided.'
