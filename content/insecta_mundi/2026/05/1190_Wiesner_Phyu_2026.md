abstract: '<i>Pronyssa horii</i> Wiesner and Phyu, new species and <i>Cylindera</i> (<i>Ifasina</i>) <i>suui</i> Wiesner and Phyu, new species (Coleoptera: Cicindelidae) are described. <i>Pronyssa horii</i> may be recognized by labral and elytral color as well as elytral maculatation. <i>Cylindera</i> (<i>I.</i>) <i>suui</i> may be recognized by its body size, shape of labrum, aedeagus and elytra, and elytral maculation. A key for the members of genus <i>Pronyssa</i> Bates, 1874, and a key for the members of the subgenus <i>Ifasina</i> Jeannel, 1946 of genus <i>Cylindera</i> Westwood, 1831 from Myanmar are provided.'
