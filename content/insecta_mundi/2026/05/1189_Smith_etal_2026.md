abstract: 'The sixteen extant genera of Cybocephalidae (Coleoptera) occurring worldwide are keyed. The genera included are <i>Amedissia</i> Kirejtshuk and Mantič, <i>Apastillus</i> Kirejtshuk and Mantič, <i>Conglobatus</i> T. R. Smith, <i>Cybocephalus</i> Erichson, <i>Endrodiellus</i> Endrödy-Younga, <i>Eupastillus</i> Lawrence, <i>Hierronius</i> Endrödy-Younga, <i>Horadion</i> Endrödy-Younga, <i>Microthomas</i> T. R. Smith, <i>Pacicephalus</i> Kirejtshuk and Mantič, <i>Pastillodes</i> Endrödy-Younga, <i>Pastillus</i> Endrödy-Younga, <i>Pycnocephalus</i> Sharp, <i>Taxicephomerus</i> Kirejtshuk, <i>Theticephalus</i> Kirejtshuk and the new genus <i>Novenariacia</i> T. R. Smith and Poorani. <i>Novenariacia</i> is described along with one new species, <i>N. sulcata</i> T. R. Smith and Poorani, from India. Descriptions of <i>N. sulcata</i> adults and larvae, photographs and illustrations of morphological features, biological notes and distribution data are provided.'
