abstract: 'The known species of the <i>tuberosus </i>group of the genus <i>Therates </i>Latreille (Coleoptera: Cicindelidae) are redescribed. <i>Therates rugosoangustoides </i>Wiesner and Anichtchenko, new species, is described from northern Vietnam while <i>T. horni </i>Wiesner, 1988, new status, and <i>T. pseudotuberosus </i>Wiesner, 1988, new status, are elevated to species rank. Identification keys for the twelve groups of the genus <i>Therates </i>and for the eight species of the <i>tuberosus </i>group are provided. Lectotypes are designated for <i>Therates tuberosus </i>Fleutiaux, 1893, <i>Therates angustatus </i>Horn, 1902, <i>Therates crebrepunctatus </i>Horn, 1923, and <i>Therates rugosoangustus </i>Horn, 1929, to stabilize their nomenclature to single specimens.'
