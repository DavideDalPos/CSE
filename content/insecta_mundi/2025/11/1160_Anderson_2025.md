abstract: 'Several changes in classification are made in Nearctic taxa of the tribe Byrsopagini (Coleoptera: Curculionidae: Entiminae). The genus <i>Pseudorimus </i>Van Dyke, 1934 is here placed as a new junior subjective synonym of <i>Crocidema </i>Van Dyke, 1934. This results in <i>Crocidema granicollis </i>(Van Dyke, 1934), new combination and <i>Crocidema orbicollis </i>(Van Dyke, 1934), new combination. In addition, the Mexican species <i>Amphidees latifrons </i>(Sharp, 1891) is here transferred to <i>Crocidema </i>as <i>Crocidema latifrons </i>(Sharp, 1891), new combination. The genus <i>Melanolemma </i>Van Dyke, 1935 is here placed as a new junior subjective synonym of <i>Peritaxia </i>Horn, 1876. This results in <i>Peritaxia montana </i>(Van Dyke, 1935), new combination. <i>Crocidema attenuata </i>Van Dyke, 1934 is here placed as a species of <i>Peritaxia </i>as <i>Peritaxia attenuata </i>(Van Dyke, 1934), new combination. <i>Peritaxia brevipilis </i>Van Dyke, 1935 is removed from <i>Peritaxia </i>and placed in <i>Dichoxenus </i>as <i>Dichoxenus brevipilis </i>(Van Dyke), new combination. <i>Plinthodes </i>LeConte, 1876 and <i>Triglyphulus </i>Cockerell, 1906 are here placed as new junior subjective synonyms of <i>Trichalophus </i>LeConte, 1876. This results in <i>Trichalophus foveirostris </i>(Chittenden, 1925), new combination, <i>Trichalophus taeniatus </i>(LeConte. 1857), new combination, <i>Trichalophus ater </i>(LeConte, 1876), new combination and <i>Trichalophus nevadensis </i>(Van Dyke, 1938), new combination. <i>Dirotognathus </i>Horn, 1876 is here placed as a new junior subjective synonym of <i>Lepidophorus </i>Kirby, 1837. This results in <i>Lepidophorus sordidus </i>(Horn, 1876), new combination and <i>Lepidophorus punctatus </i>(Hatch, 1971), new combination. New lectotypes are designated for <i>Deamphus latifrons </i>Sharp, 1891, <i>Peritaxia hispida </i>Horn, 1876, <i>Peritaxia rugicollis </i>Horn, 1876 and <i>Dirotognathus sordidus </i>Horn, 1876'
