abstract: 'The millipede genus <i>Apterourus </i>Loomis, 1966, the only genus of the family Apterouridae Loomis, 1966 (Diplopoda: Chordeumatida: Striarioidea), contains two species and is rarely collected. We add a third species from Mt. Palomar, San Diego County, California, USA, <i>Apterourus palomar </i>Shear, Richart and Marek, new species.'
