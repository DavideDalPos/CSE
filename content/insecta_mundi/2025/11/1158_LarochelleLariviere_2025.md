abstract: 'The subtribe Actenonycina (Coleoptera: Carabidae: Harpalinae: Odacanthini) is revised for New Zealand. Two genera and six species are recognized. One genus and three species are described as new: <i>Neactenonyx </i>Larochelle and Lariviere new genus, <i>Neactenonyx mcfarlani </i>Larochelle and Lariviere new species, <i>Neactenonyx sharpi </i>Larochelle and Lariviere new species, <i>Actenonyx whitei </i>Larochelle and Lariviere new species. One new combination is established: <i>Actenonyx aotearoa </i>Kavanaugh, 2022 = <i>Neactenonyx aotearoa </i>(Kavanaugh, 2022). <i>Sphallax peryphoides </i>Bates, 1871, previously synonymized with <i>Actenonyx bembidioides </i>White, 1846, is reinstated as a full species. A revision of all taxa is provided. Descriptions, identification keys, illustrations of male genitalia, habitus photos, distributional data and maps are given. Information on ecology, biology, dispersal power, and collecting techniques is included for each species.'
