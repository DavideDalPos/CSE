abstract: 'This revision treats species of the genus <i>Isclzyrus </i>Lacordaire 1842, <i>sensu </i>Crotch 1876 <i>(Micrisclzyrus </i>Alvarenga 1965), occurring from Panama north. A key to species, descriptions and illustrations of terms used, habitus and genitalic illustrations for all species, distribution maps, detailed species descriptions, complete synonymies, label data from specimens studied, and comparisons with similar species are presented. A list of all names used in combination with <i>lschyrus </i>and their current status is presented to prevent future homonyms. This revision treats 45 species: 26 previously described species plus 19 new species: <i>lschyrus audbalus, I. ceIatus, I. cordiformis, I, erosus, I. goliai, I. ichnus, I. impressus, I. joplini, I. kovariki, I. malleus, I. mimus, I. monochromatus, I. mystacis, </i><i>I. parallelus, I. parcarum, I. poseidon, I. putei, I. pyropodus, I. sapphirus. </i>Nine names were found to be junior synonyms: <i>I. quinquepimctatus </i>Gorham 1887 placed under <i>I. angularis </i>Lacordaire 1842; <i>Episcaphula bellopicta </i>Kuhnt 1910 placed under <i>I. auriculatus </i>Lacordaire 1842; <i>I. peruvianus </i>Gorham 1883 placed under <i>I. boucardi </i>Crotch 1876; <i>I. gratiosus </i>Guerin-Meneville 1844 placed under <i>I. incertus </i>Lacordaire 1842; <i>I. subcylindricus </i>Lacordaire 1842, <i>I. graphicus </i>Lacordaire 1842, and <i>I. puncticollis </i>Gorham 1887 placed under <i>I. quadripunctatus quadripunctatus </i>(Olivier 1792); <i>Dacne femoralis </i>Chevrolat 1841 and <i>I. velatus </i>Lacordaire 1842 placed under <i>I. scriptus </i>(Olivier 1801). <i>lschyrus chiasticus </i>Boyle 1954 is reduced 10 a subspecies of <i>I. quadripunctatus </i>(Olivier 1792). Lectotypes were designated for 23 names: <i>Dacne femoralis </i>Chevrolat, <i>Episcaphula bellopicta </i>Kuhnt, <i>Ischyrus agnatus </i>Crotch, <i>I. angularis </i>Lacordaire, <i>I. auriculatus </i>Lacordaire, <i>I. bogotae </i>Crotch, <i>I. boucardi </i>Crotch, <i>I. collatinus </i>Crotch, <i>I. distinguendus </i>Lacordaire, <i>I. elegantulus </i>Lacordaire, <i>I. ephippiatus </i>Gorham, <i>I. frolltalis </i>Lacordaire, <i>I. graphicus </i>Lacordaire, <i>I. gratiosus </i>Guerin-Meneville, <i>I. insolens </i>Crotch, <i>I. maculiventris </i>Lacordaire, <i>I. peruvianus </i>Gorham, <i>I. proximus </i>Lacordaire, <i>I. septemsignatus </i>Gorham, <i>I. subcylindricus </i>Lacordaire, <i>I</i>.<i>tripunctatus </i>Crotch, <i>I. velatus </i>Lacordaire, <i>I. vespertilio </i>Lacordaire.'
