abstract: 'The genus <i>Typhlodromus Scheuren </i>is here revised and keyed to species. Two new subgenera, <i>Trionus </i>and <i>Oudemanus </i>are herein described and 27 species are illustrated, including 3 new species: <i>Typhlodromus knisleyi, Typhlodromus quercicolus, </i>and <i>Typhlodromus zaheri. </i>The following new synonomies are proposed: <i>Typhlodromus perbibus </i>Wainstein and Arutunjan as a junior synonym of <i>Typhlodromus athiasae </i>Porath and Swirski; <i>Typhlodromus exhilaratus </i>Ragusa as a junior synonym of <i>Typhlodromus tiliae </i>Oudemans; and <i>Typhlodromus eucervix </i>Karg and Edland as a junior synonym of <i>Typhlodromus andrei </i>Karg. The plant hosts, food hosts, and distributions are discussed.'
